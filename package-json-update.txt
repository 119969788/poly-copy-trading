在 package.json 的 "scripts" 部分添加以下两行：

    "batch-sell": "tsx src/batch-sell.ts",
    "batch-sell-real": "tsx src/batch-sell.ts --real",

完整的 scripts 部分应该是：

  "scripts": {
    "start": "tsx src/index.ts",
    "dev": "tsx watch src/index.ts",
    "build": "tsc",
    "typecheck": "tsc --noEmit",
    "batch-sell": "tsx src/batch-sell.ts",
    "batch-sell-real": "tsx src/batch-sell.ts --real"
  },
